Welcome to CCIC Lab at IIT Madras

Welcome to the Computational Catalysis and Interfacial Chemistry (CCIC) research group led by Dr. Sooraj Kunnikuruvan. Our group focuses on understanding and designing catalytic and interfacial processes through advanced computational techniques. We investigate a wide range of chemical reactions-both homogeneous and heterogeneous-that occur at various interfaces, with applications in sustainable fuel production, energy storage systems such as batteries, and broader energy conversion technologies. Our research combines first-principles methods like density functional theory (DFT), ab initio molecular dynamics (AIMD), and classical molecular dynamics (CMD) to uncover mechanistic insights and guide experimental efforts. We also integrate machine learning (ML) and data-driven approaches to accelerate the discovery and optimization of catalytic materials and interfacial chemistries. Through this multidisciplinary approach, we aim to address fundamental challenges in energy sustainability and contribute to the development of next-generation catalytic and energy storage technologies.
