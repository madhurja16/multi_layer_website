Our research group at the Department of Chemistry, IIT Madras, focuses on **theoretical and computational chemistry** with a keen interest in **catalysis** and **interfacial phenomena**. We employ methods like Density Functional Theory (DFT), Ab-initio Molecular Dynamics (AIMD), and Machine Learning to study complex chemical systems critical for sustainable energy, material science, and chemical synthesis.
